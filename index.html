About

The West-Mec Aviation Maintenance program is not only a 147 aviation school but also a Federal Aviation Administration(FAA) goverened school. This means that all of the hours included are manditory and nessesary for completion. Here at West-Mec Aviation we train the next generation of students to have the skills to be successfull Airframe technicians, powerplant technicians, air transportation technicians. The skills the are taught are not limited to these careers are can be used in many other trades.

CTSO

Our CTSO is SkillsUSA. SkillsUSA is a partnership of students, teachers and industry working together to ensure America has a skilled workforce. Their main goal is to help students excel. SkillsUSA serves more than 360,000 students and instructors annually. SKillsUSA has served more than 13.5 million members since 1965. Our local chapter has won 1st, 2nd, and 8th in nationals in the past three years. Also we win 1st, 2nd, and 3rd in state every year.

SkillsUSA focuses on building the three framework skills which are Technical, Personal, and Workplace. This framework provides a common language for students to articulate what they gain from SkillsUSA to possible employers. It allows students to assesses thier develpment in the skills they learn. It empowers students to seize control of thier own learning to achieve a successful career. Most importantly it gives students a skill set that it demanded by business and industry but lacking in many employees today.
