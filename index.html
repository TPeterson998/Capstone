CTSO

Our CTSO is SkillsUSA. SkillsUSA is a partnership of students, teachers and industry working together to ensure America has a skilled workforce. Their main goal is to help students excel. SkillsUSA serves more than 360,000 students and instructors annually. SKillsUSA has served more than 13.5 million members since 1965. Our local chapter ......

SkillsUSA focuses on building the three framework skills which are Technical, Personal, and Workplace. This framework provides a common language for students to articulate what they gain from SkillsUSA to possible employers. It allows students to assesses thier develpment in the skills they learn. It empowers students to seize control of thier own learning to achieve a successful career. Most importantly it gives students a skill set that it demanded by business and industry but lacking in many employees today.
